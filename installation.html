Step-by-Step Installation Guide

  1. Assess Your Needs and Site: Calculate your water requirements and measure your rooftop catchment area. Consider local rainfall patterns and storage capacity needs.
  2. Design Your System: Plan for gutters, downspouts, filters, storage tanks, and overflow systems. Choose durable, non-toxic materials appropriate for your environment and intended water use.
  3. Prepare the Catchment Area: Clean your rooftop to remove debris and contaminants. Install or adjust gutters and downspouts to ensure effective collection and conveyance.
  4. Install Filtration: Add mesh filters and first-flush diverters to exclude debris and initial dirty runoff from entering the storage tank.
  5. Set Up Storage Tanks: Position tanks on stable stands. Connect pipes securely to guide water into tanks, allowing easy access for maintenance and water use.
  6. Install Overflow and Recharge Systems: Ensure overflow pipes divert excess water safely. Optionally add recharge pits or wells to replenish groundwater.
  7. Maintenance: Regularly clean catchment areas, gutters, filters, and tanks. Inspect and repair leaks or blockages to maintain water quality and system efficiency effectively.
