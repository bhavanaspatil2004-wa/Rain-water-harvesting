What is Rainwater Harvesting?

Rainwater harvesting means collecting and storing rainwater for use at home or outdoors, instead of letting it drain away. It's an eco-friendly practice that eases pressure on traditional water supplies, supports groundwater recharge, and helps reduce flooding. This method protects the environment and encourages wise water use in every community.
